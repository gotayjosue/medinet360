Manage patient records effortlessly.

Medinet360 helps healthcare professionals organize, track, and access patient data — all in one secure platform.

Add. Update. Delete

Keep your patients' information organized and always up to date. Easily add, update, or delete records while maintaining accurate medical histories. Streamline your workflow and focus on what truly matters — providing quality care to your patients.

Schedule. Manage.

Schedule, view, and manage appointments in just a few clicks. With Medinet360's calendar system, you can coordinate schedules, receive automated reminders, and prevent booking conflicts, ensuring a smooth and efficient patient experience.

Generate. View.

Gain clear insights into your clinic's performance. Generate detailed reports on appointments, patients, and overall activity. Make informed decisions with easy-to-understand analytics and visual summaries.

Custom Fields

Tailor the platform to fit your clinic's specific needs. Create custom fields to record unique patient information and enhance your data management with details that truly support better medical care.

Assistants

Empower your medical team with collaborative access. Medinet360 allows assistants to create their own accounts and join a clinic by entering the clinic's unique ID. Once approved, assistants can help manage patient records, schedule appointments, and handle daily administrative tasks — keeping your clinic organized and your patients well cared for.
