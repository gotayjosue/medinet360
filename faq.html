FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Medinet360?

Medinet360 is a cloud-based clinic management platform designed to simplify patient registration, appointment scheduling, and medical record management. It helps healthcare professionals and their assistants keep everything organized in one secure and easy-to-use system.

Who can use Medinet360?

Medinet360 is ideal for doctors, clinics, and healthcare professionals of any specialty. Assistants and administrative staff can also create accounts to help manage patient records and appointments.

Is my data secure on Medinet360?

Yes. We use advanced encryption and secure cloud hosting to protect all patient and clinic information. Only authorized users have access to their clinic’s data.

Can assistants access the same information as doctors?

Assistants have limited access according to the permissions set by the doctor. They can help with administrative tasks such as creating or updating patient records and scheduling appointments, without accessing sensitive medical information.

Can I customize Medinet360 to fit my clinic’s needs?

Yes. Medinet360 lets you fully personalize the way you register patient information. You can create custom templates with your own fields, sections, and formats — allowing you to collect exactly the data your clinic needs. This flexibility ensures every record is consistent, organized, and perfectly aligned with your workflow.

Does Medinet360 offer reports and analytics?

Yes. The platform provides detailed reports on appointments, patients, and overall clinic performance. These insights help you make data-driven decisions and improve operational efficiency.
